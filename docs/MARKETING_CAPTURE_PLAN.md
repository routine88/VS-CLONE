# Marketing Capture Plan

This plan sequences the capture sessions needed for the vertical slice reveal and press kit. Each beat is scripted so gameplay
footage, stills, and copy stay in lockstep with the current polish backlog.

## Scripted Gameplay Beats

1. **Opening Descent (0:00 – 0:20)**
   - Capture hunter spawn animation, environmental pan of Graveyard biome, and UI boot sequence.
   - Show controller input overlay to reinforce console readiness.
2. **First Swarm Engagement (0:20 – 0:50)**
   - Demonstrate crowd-control weapon (Scythe Arc) clearing initial wave.
   - Highlight hit feedback and hazard telegraphs.
3. **Glyph Unlock & Upgrade Loop (0:50 – 1:40)**
   - Earn first level, present upgrade choices, select Rapid Fire chain.
   - Trigger glyph unlock animation and ultimate charge meter.
4. **Hazard Showcase (1:40 – 2:20)**
   - Force-trigger Grave Wisp miasma cloud, transition to barricade salvage.
   - Include UI callouts from localization set (EN/ES/DE/FR).
5. **Miniboss Duel (2:20 – 3:00)**
   - Introduce miniboss entrance VO, demonstrate dash/ultimate cadence.
   - Capture slow-motion finisher for hero shot.
6. **Dawn Breaks Outro (3:00 – 3:20)**
   - Show survival banner, loot summary, and runtime playback overlay.

## Press-Kit Copy (Draft)

**Headline:** “Nightfall Survivors” invites players to master the night with precision upgrades and relentless hordes.

**Boilerplate:**
Nightfall Survivors is a top-down survival roguelite where every sunrise is earned. Build your hunter by chaining glyph-driven
ultimates, salvage barricades to push the frontline, and leverage hazards to turn the tide. The vertical slice highlights the
Graveyard biome, Rapid Fire weapon track, and full controller support as the team marches toward Early Access.

**Key Messages:**
- Controller-first design with seamless keyboard fallback.
- Fully localized onboarding (English, Spanish, German, French) for global playtests.
- Deterministic runtime pipeline guarantees repeatable runs for content creators.

**Call to Action:**
Wishlist on Steam and join the community survey at launch.nightfallsurvivors.com.
